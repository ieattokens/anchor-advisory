Finance background, builder mentality

Anchor Advisory was founded on a simple observation: most businesses don't have a reporting problem or a tools problem. They have a trust problem. The numbers exist, but nobody's confident enough to make decisions from them.

I came up through the finance side: early-stage startups where the work was securing lines of credit and supporting private equity raises, then growth-stage operations building revenue recognition models, operating models, pricing frameworks, and multi-state sales tax programs from the ground up. More recently, enterprise environments where the challenge is connecting multiple reporting systems to shared sources so that leadership gets consistent, timely data instead of conflicting spreadsheets.

That progression shapes how I work. I'm not an advisor who hands you a deck. I get inside your systems, write the queries, build the models, and deliver infrastructure that holds up after I leave.
